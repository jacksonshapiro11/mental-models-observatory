# Growth Strategy

**Build audience → Community → Your writing**

---

## Sequence

1. **Website** ✅ (119 models, done)
2. **Twitter** (automated, reach new people)
3. **Newsletter** (capture, deepen, your voice)
4. **Your writing** (differentiate, authority)
5. **Reddit** (community, when ready)

---

## Newsletter

**Format**: Your intro + Model + Highlight + CTA  
**Length**: ~300 words  
**Frequency**: Weekly  
**Your voice**: Intro only (philosophy, themes)  
**AI**: Distills content, keeps concise

**Run**: `npm run newsletter`

---

## Your Writing

**Content**: Book reviews extracting models + your synthesis  
**Where**: Your site + Medium  
**Frequency**: 1/week to start  
**Differentiator**: Your unique perspective, model combinations

---

## Community

**When**: After you have content + audience  
**Where**: Reddit (join existing or create)  
**Why**: Discussion, applications, user stories

---

## Key Principle

**Quality > Quantity. Your voice matters. Community amplifies what exists.**

---

**Focus**: Newsletter first. Then writing. Then community.
